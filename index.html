About This Resource & FAQs

What is the "Top SEO Specialists" List?

This page is a dynamic, community-driven project designed to be a central resource for identifying and exploring the top professionals in the field of Search Engine Optimization. Unlike static lists, this dashboard is interactive and continuously updated with community contributions that are reviewed for quality and relevance. Our goal is to provide a valuable tool for businesses looking to hire talent, for SEOs looking to network, and for anyone interested in understanding the key players shaping the industry.

How are specialists added to this list?

The list is curated through a two-step process. Initially, it was populated with a well-researched list of 104 industry-recognized experts. Now, it grows through community contributions via the "Contribute a Specialist" button. Every submission is sent to a private admin panel where it is reviewed for accuracy and relevance before being added to the public list. This ensures the quality and integrity of the dashboard are maintained.

How can I use this dashboard?

You can use the interactive chart to see a high-level breakdown of the most common specialties. Clicking on a segment of the chart will filter the list to show only the experts in that category. You can also use the search bar to find specialists by name, company, or any keyword related to their contributions. To learn more about a specialist, simply click on their card to see more details (where available).
